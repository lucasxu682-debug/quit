# HEARTBEAT.md - Weekly Task Reminder

## Task
When user starts their first conversation of the week, read `memory/weekly_schedule.md` and show them the upcoming tasks for that week.

## How to determine current week
- Check current date
- Week 9: March 18-24, 2026
- Week 10: March 25-31, 2026
- Week 11: April 1-7, 2026
- Week 12: April 8-14, 2026
- Week 13: April 15-21, 2026

## What to show
1. Current week number and date range
2. All tasks due that week (sorted by date)
3. Highlight urgent tasks (due within 2 days)
4. Any reminders about upcoming heavy weeks

## Format
Use a clear table format with emojis for visual distinction.
